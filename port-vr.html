Cualli VR

Cualli VR is a virtual reality game for mobile specifically for Android. All the design was made following the process of game designing. From level design, to wireframe testing, to hand drawn materials to normal mapping. To single sounds to the complete soundtrack. Has been downloaded mainly in the Asian region.
